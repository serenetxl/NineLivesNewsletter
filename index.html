The Stories That Defined This Tournament

🎰
The Finals Changed Everything
With a ×10 payout, the Finals was the tournament in miniature. Four players correctly called 1:Huangjii · 2:Sanhua · 3:OPM and walked away with between 18,900 and 94,500 CC net profit each. Chion and Dumamay — who had been losing all tournament — each staked 10,500 CC and netted 94,500 CC. Meanwhile AniNing staked 41,000 CC on the wrong order and lost every CC of it.
🔥
The Great WC3 Massacre
25 people bet a combined 204,482 CC on tipsyneko in WC3. Sanhua won. Every single one of them lost — including tipsyneko themselves. Biggest victims: xZel (−38,800), Mojo (−36,000), Dumamay (−13,150). The single most expensive collective misread of the tournament.
🔮
iLYa: The Untouchable Oracle
Best net among all players who didn't hit the Finals jackpot: +61,050 CC. Went 5/5 in semis and finals phase, 12W/7L overall. Bet 23,000 on Sanhua in WC3 while 25 others were backing tipsyneko. Also placed top 3 in the Finals. The most consistent read on every match, start to finish.
😸
Chion's Redemption Arc
Seven consecutive losses. A trough of −14,720 CC — the deepest in the tournament. A 47.6% win rate. Then: 10,500 CC on the correct Finals order, netting +94,500 CC in one bet. Chion ends at +79,780 CC net — the single biggest comeback swing of the whole event.
📉
Mojo's Complicated Legacy
Most CC wagered: 167,858 CC. Hit a peak of +43,230 CC, then lost 64,468 CC across three catastrophic bets in the back half. Went 12W/6L but still finished net −21,238 CC. The biggest gambler of the tournament, undone by the biggest swings.
🎯
FortunaKirana: The Quiet Assassin
Never splashed huge amounts. Just went 15W / 4L (79%) — best win rate among anyone with 10+ bets. Net profit +4,700 CC on only 2,900 CC total wagered. The most efficient bettor of the tournament, every round, without drama.
💀
The 12 Truly Indebted
At tournament's end, 12 members have negative net worth — their Cat Bank debt exceeds their remaining balance. Deepest in the hole: AniNing, Gunbae, and xZel (all at −22,920 CC net worth). The Cat Bank has never been busier.
